Municipal Bonds Program

REFACTOR: Stablecoin Community Bonds for Rent Relief

Why Stablecoins, Not Bitcoin

  • Bitcoin is volatile (50-80% crashes) - stablecoins are 1:1 with USD
  • No capital gains tax complications
  • 24/7 settlement via blockchain
  • Low minimums ($25-100) - accessible to all New Yorkers

Precedents

  • Quincy, MA - $10M blockchain bond (2024)
  • Hong Kong - HK$800M tokenized green bond

Community Wealth Model

  • Enable lower-income residents to buy muni bonds
  • Yield-bearing investment for working class
  • Returns fund rent control relief
  • Builds community stake in city's fiscal health

Why Crypto Tax Payments Fail

Economic Disincentives

  • Fees (1-2%) higher than free ACH transfers
  • Spending crypto triggers capital gains tax
  • Strong returns discourage spending
  • Exchange fees lower than payment processors

Behavioral Reality

  • Crypto viewed as investment, not payment
  • "HODL" mentality dominates
  • Limited awareness of option
  • No benefit vs. traditional payment
"Paying taxes in crypto was never going to be mainstream — it's a symbolic gesture, not a real use case. Most holders see crypto as a long-term asset, not a checking account."
- Mike Cahill, Douro Labs CEO
