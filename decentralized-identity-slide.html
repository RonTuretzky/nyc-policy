Proposal 5

Decentralized Identity (DIDs)

Allocate governance weight for community decisions based on verified volunteer hours—putting power in the hands of active participants.

Specific Use Cases

  • Mural design decisions with neighborhood influence
  • Participatory budgeting applications
  • Funding allocation decisions
  • Community capital formation

Related Tools

Sarafu Commitment Pooling Gardens Conviction Voting Octant Vaults Flow State Funding Hypercerts

Key Principle

Governance weight should be earned through community participation, not purchased with capital. DIDs enable verifiable credentials without centralized databases.
