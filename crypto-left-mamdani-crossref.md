### Quote from Blockchain Radicals

> "The majority of venture capital backed web3 startups claim to be 'decentralized' when the only thing decentralized about them is the fact they use a blockchain's technically decentralized infrastructure. They are, however, not owned in a decentralized way... From a leftist perspective, markets under capitalism are essentially 'centralization machines for capital.'"

— The Blockchain Socialist ([Source](https://theblockchainsocialist.com/crypto-leftist-heuristics-what-is-a-dao/))

This critique aligns perfectly with Mamdani's anti-billionaire, pro-worker platform. The Crypto Left movement offers an alternative vision where blockchain serves cooperative, worker-owned, and community-controlled institutions.
