## Overview

The Use Case Lab is an applied research initiative at the Ethereum Foundation focused on high-impact use cases for Ethereum through collaborative prototyping, pilots, and standards.

The **Verifiable Cities track** examines where coordination and verifiability constraints are most acute in urban systems, and where public blockchains can meaningfully expand civic and state capacity.

### Core Thesis

Cities are increasingly expected to deliver public services with greater effectiveness and accountability. Rather than introduce new centralized or siloed platforms, blockchain can **embed transparency, accountability, and coordination capabilities directly into how public rules and finances operate**.

This creates "credibility environments" around coordination and government claims—without requiring new control or adding administrative overhead. The goal is to enable a shared digital **"trust layer"** across cities that extends accountability from administrative processes to **transparent technical mechanisms**.

---

## Why Cities? (4 Hypotheses)

1. **Proximity** - Cities are the primary institutional level where coordination and service delivery must happen in real time with proximity to concerned presences

2. **Pragmatism** - Cities face immediate service demands, operate across dense networks of public and private actors, and contend with real implications and constraints requiring equally pragmatic solutions

3. **Replicability** - Despite variation in competitive pressures and context, thousands of municipal governments and service providers worldwide face common operational needs, creating clear but currently underleveraged opportunities for experimentation and knowledge transfer

4. **Leverage** - Together these conditions make cities powerful levers for public sector innovation, offering a scalable pathway for modernizing civic and state capacity

## Use Case Landscape

### Historical Trajectory

| Era | Characteristics |
|-----|-----------------|
| **Paper Government** | Public trust and legitimacy of institutions built through constitutional safeguards and formal administrative procedures |
| **Early Digitization** | Cloud platform adoption and vendor dependencies emerging |
| **Digitization Era (Present)** | Trust relies on institutional legitimacy and procedural safeguards; supervised by Department IT/Modernization |
| **Coordination Era (Future)** | Trust supported by transparent, verifiable technical mechanisms embedded directly into public rules, systems and finances |

- Aging infrastructure

- Land use & urbanization pressures

## Call for Implementations

The Use Case Lab is issuing an **open call for implementations** oriented toward collaboration with city and regional governments, as well as other funders and organizations engaged in municipal innovation.

### Priority Criteria

- Narrowly scoped implementations
- Aware of real-world deployment considerations

1. **Sandboxed/Scoped Pilots** - Controlled experiments with limited scope

## Select Implementation Examples

### 1. Dynamic Zoning & Land Use

**Problem:** Complex zoning regulations require manual compliance verification

**Solution:**
- Automated on-chain compliance checks
- Real-time land use updates
- For sensor data, drones, real-time conditions, etc.

**NYC Relevance:** NYC's complex zoning environment could benefit from transparent, verifiable enforcement

---

### 2. Benefits & Service Delivery

**Problem:** Aid delivery lacks transparency and impact measurement

**Solution:**
- Verifiable tracking of aid delivery to recipients
- Transparent impact measurement
- Proof of delivery and utilization

**NYC Relevance:** NYC's vast social services system could demonstrate outcomes more credibly

---

### 3. Programmable Climate Policy

**Problem:** Carbon credit systems lack verification and automation

**Solution:**
- Smart contracts for carbon credits
- Automated monitoring and verification
- Programmable incentive structures

---

### 4. Public Procurement & Outcomes-based Contracting

**Problem:** Procurement processes lack transparency; outcomes hard to verify

**Solution:**
- Verification of procurement processes
- Outcomes-based payment triggers
- Transparent vendor selection

---

### 5. EV Charging Network Coordination

**Problem:** Multiple EV charging providers lack coordination; billing disputes common

**Solution:**
- Real-time coordination of EV charging across multiple providers
- Verifiable billing and settlement
- Load balancing across the grid

**NYC Relevance:** Highly relevant for NYC's growing EV infrastructure rollout

---

### 6. Treasury Settlement for City Obligations

**Problem:** Municipal payments take days to settle; lack real-time visibility

**Solution:**
- Real-time settlement and tracking of payments via blockchain
- Instant, verifiable payments for contractors and vendors
- Reduced float and reconciliation costs

**NYC Relevance:** Could apply to NYC's $100B+ annual spending

---

### 7. Municipal Investment & Transparent Fiscal Reporting

**Problem:** Municipal investments and finances lack real-time public visibility

**Solution:**
- On-chain tracking of municipal investments
- Real-time financial reporting
- Public accountability dashboards

## Relevance to NYC Policy

### High-Priority Use Cases for NYC

1. **EV Charging Coordination** - NYC's growing EV infrastructure
2. **Benefits & Service Delivery** - NYC's extensive social services
3. **Treasury Settlement** - $100B+ annual city spending
4. **Dynamic Zoning** - NYC's complex zoning environment
5. **Participatory Budgeting** - Building on Harlem District 9 pilot

### Recommended Actions

1. **Engage EF Use Case Lab** as a strategic partner for implementation pilots
2. **Apply for open call** with scoped NYC-specific pilots
3. **Leverage global precedents** (CA DMV, Buenos Aires) for implementation guidance
4. **Adopt "trust layer" framing** - embed verification into existing processes rather than building new platforms

### Key Insight

The Verifiable Cities approach aligns with progressive crypto policy: rather than industry promotion or speculation-focused initiatives, it emphasizes **genuine public utility** through transparent, verifiable government operations that serve residents.
