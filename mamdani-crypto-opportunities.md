# Mamdani Policies: Cryptocurrency & Blockchain Opportunities

This document analyzes Zohran Mamdani's policy positions and identifies where cryptocurrency, blockchain, or Web3 technologies could potentially advance his stated goals—even though he has been personally skeptical of the crypto industry.

---

## Executive Summary

Despite Mamdani's critical stance on cryptocurrency ([CryptoSlate](https://cryptoslate.com/zohran-mamdanis-victory-tests-nycs-crypto-identity/)), many of his policy goals align well with blockchain's core capabilities: transparency, decentralization, anti-corruption, financial inclusion for underserved communities, and reducing reliance on traditional financial gatekeepers. The irony is that the same "cypherpunk" ethos that birthed Bitcoin—distrust of concentrated power, advocacy for the marginalized, transparency in governance—resonates with Mamdani's democratic socialist platform ([Coinage Media](https://www.coinage.media/s4/billionaires-couldnt-stop-mamdani-heres-why-that-could-be-good-for-crypto)).

## Recommended Framing for Mamdani Administration

Rather than "cryptocurrency" or "blockchain," frame these solutions as:

| Avoid | Use Instead | Rationale |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| Crypto | Digital public infrastructure | Avoids negative connotations from FTX, speculation ([CryptoSlate](https://cryptoslate.com/zohran-mamdanis-victory-tests-nycs-crypto-identity/)) |
| Blockchain | Tamper-proof public records | Emphasizes transparency and accountability |
| NFTs | Digital credentials | Focuses on practical use case |
| Tokens | Community investment shares | Emphasizes democratization of finance |
| DeFi | Financial inclusion technology | Focuses on serving unbanked communities |
| Web3 | Privacy-preserving civic technology | Emphasizes protection from hostile actors |
