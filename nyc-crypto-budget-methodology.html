$850K - $2.45M

Proposed but not implemented | Based on Colorado/Miami benchmarks

Annual Operating Costs

Conversion/Volatility Buffer$50,000 - $150,000
Marketing/Education$50,000 - $100,000
TOTAL YEAR 1$850,000 - $2,450,000

Crypto Tax Pilot - Sources

Benchmark Sources:
  • Colorado Crypto Tax Program: Launched Sept 2022, PayPal partnership
  • Miami MiamiCoin: $21M treasury (largely collapsed)
  • Ohio (Defunct): OhioCrypto.com shut down 2019 after low adoption

Colorado Results

  • Fewer than 80 payments in 3 years
  • Minimal revenue impact
  • Uses PayPal (instant USD conversion)
  • No city bears crypto volatility risk

NYC Considerations

  • Much larger tax base = higher implementation cost
  • Capital gains tax on crypto payments
  • Low expected adoption based on precedent
  • Stablecoins may be more practical
Key Finding: Colorado's extremely low adoption (~80 payments over 3 years) suggests this is low priority for NYC. Cost-benefit ratio is questionable.
