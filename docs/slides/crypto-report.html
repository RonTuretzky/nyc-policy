Office of Digital Assets & Blockchain

  • Established: October 2025 via Executive Order 57
  • First municipal office of its kind in the nation
  • Reports to CTO within OTI
  • Estimated budget: $736K - $1.79M annually

Responsibilities

  • Foster crypto/blockchain ecosystem development
  • Coordinate industry-government efforts
  • Promote financial inclusion for unbanked
  • Educate public on scams and fraud
